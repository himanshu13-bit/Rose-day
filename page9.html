Saadgi

Saadgi se bhari hai wo,
Kuch kehte hai pari hai wo.
Uski chamak se lagta hai ki,
Chand ki kaarigari hai wo.

Dhadkano ki dhadak usse hai,
Ghungruon ki khanak usse hai.
Usne heere pehn ke bataya,
Ki heero ki bhi chamak usse hai.

Uski khidki ko door se,
Hoke bekaraar dekhte hai.
Dekhta hai jab usse toh,
Zaar zaar dekhte hai.

Badi mashakkat se usse laate hai khwab mai,
Fir ussi khwab ko sabhi baar baar dekhte hai.

Shauk se shuru hoti hai wo,
Fir jab uski aadat padh jaati hai.
Nasha hota hai uska iss kadar,
Fir sharab hosh mai laati hai.

Shayaro ko karti wash mai,
Najme ghazale likhwati hai.
Duniya tab eid manati hai,
Jab wo khidki pe aati hai.

Noor jiska taaj hai,
Apsaraye jiski daasi hai.
Andhere ka vilom hai wo,
Haseen ka paryayvachi hai.

Wo hai samundar, kinara bhi hai,
Wo hai khwahish, sitaara bhi hai.
Ye mera munafa khasara bhi hai,
Ki wo mera bhi hai aur pyara bhi hai.
